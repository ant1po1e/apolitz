About Apolitz

With this tool, you can make a chart or beatmap easily. You can calculate BPM automatically and the calculation nearly accurate.

You can also convert every type of audio file into the software itself (e.g: .ogg, .mp3). This tool also has waveform so you can visually see audio pitch through the editor itself.
