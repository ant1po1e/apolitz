About THE ORAL CIGARETTES

THE ORAL CIGARETTES (ジ オーラル シガレッツ) is a four-member Japanese alternative rock band from Nara Prefecture formed in July 2010. The band signed to A-Sketch in 2012 and has currently released two independent albums, three studio albums, one extended play, eight single albums, and one demo album.
